03 THE FRAME

Eduba operates on a simple rule. Most organizations automate the wrong things. The work is sorting each workflow onto the layer where it belongs.

60%
LAYER 01 · TRADITIONAL CODE & DATABASE

Parts catalogs. Test-article tracking. Supply-chain logs. The quiet infrastructure that keeps Zeus moving from propellant bench to range.

30%
LAYER 02 · RULE-BASED LOGIC

DFARS flowdowns. CMMC controls. ITAR export classifications. Partnership-agreement obligations. Rules plus documentation plus review cycles.

10%
LAYER 03 · GENUINE AI

ODIN's target identification and tracking. Anomaly detection in flight-test telemetry. Assistive narrative generation for program reporting. Proprietary work. Aeon's to keep.

The work Eduba proposes is to sit with the Aeon team and sort the current workflows into those three layers. Compliance documentation, partnership ops, and after-action knowledge capture are orchestration problems with known solutions. ODIN's AI layer stays Aeon's proprietary work. No new software. No migration. A written operating architecture the team can keep running.
